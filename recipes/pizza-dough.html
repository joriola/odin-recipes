Pizza Dough Recipe

Description

The foundation of a delicious pizza.

Enough for 4 pizza dough balls.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of cold water
  • 1 tbsp of salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp of instant dried yeast or 2 tsp of active dried yeast
  • 2 1/2 cups of all purpose flour

Steps

  1. Place 1 cup of the water in a large bowl. Bring the remaining water to a boil and add it to the bowl of cold water.
  2. Whisk the salt and yeast into the water.
  3. Place the flour in a large bowl and pour the water mixture into it. Stir with a wooden spoon until the dough comes together in a ball.
  4. Turn the dough onto a floured surface and knead for 10 minutes, until it's firm and strechy.
  5. Return the dough into the bowl, cover and leave to rise in a warm place for about 2 hours or until it has doubled in size.
  6. Divide dough into 4 equal pieces and place each piece into separate bowls. Cover and leave to rise for another 30-60 minutes or until doubled.
  7. When ready to use, on a floured surface, use floured fingertips to flatten into a disc and stretch to the size of your pizza stone.
